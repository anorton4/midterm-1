The Epic Trilogy of The Lord of the Rings

With 17 Oscars under its belt, the Lord of the Rings movie trilogy is an achievement of epic proportions. Embracing the heart of J.R.R. Tolkien's original books, the LOTR movie trilogy captured the awe and wonder of Middle-Earth, with its themes of beauty, fellowship, melancholy, and light vs dark. Journey with me as we explore how each of the 3 films has captured hearts and minds of all ages and across generations.

The Two Towers

In this second installment, our fellowship has been split into 3 groups. There is Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli who roam the wide plains of Rohan and fight alongside the Rohirrim. We have Merry and Pippin who befriend the Ents, specifically Treebeard, and help to destroy Isengard. And we have our two heroes, Frodo and Sam, who travel with Gollum on their way to Mordor. And last but not least, our favorite wizard, Gandalf, makes an epic return.
